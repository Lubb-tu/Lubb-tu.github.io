About Us

LUBB – Language Unveils Balanced Bonds

At LUBB, we believe language connects ideas in harmonious ways.

Our AI-powered tool deciphers these connections to solve verbal analogy questions, transforming complex linguistic patterns into clear, actionable insights using state-of-the-art large language models (LLMs).

Born from a team of Taif University students passionate about education, LUBB is designed to support learners in mastering verbal reasoning while advancing the frontiers of NLP. We’re not just building a tool—we’re pioneering a future where language learning is intuitive, equitable, and deeply human.
